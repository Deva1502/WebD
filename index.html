Expectation

Before entering college, we have certain expectations about what college life will be like. Some common expectations include:

  • Endless fun and partying
  • Meeting a diverse group of people
  • Easily balancing academics and a social life
  • Landing a high-paying job after graduation
  • Very Rude teachers
  • Good boys:girls ratio high
  • Easily able to make friendship with opposite gender

Reality

While college can be a great experience, the reality often differs from the expectations. Some common realities of college life include:

  • Balancing academics and a social life can be challenging
  • Making friends takes effort and time
  • Money can be tight and managing finances is important
  • Very friendly teachers
  • very less boys:girls ratio ie 1:20
  • Highly supportive seniors
  • Not able to talk even with single girl
